Overview

What is RCCMS?

The Revenue Court Case Management System (RCCMS) is a Government of India initiative under the Digital India programme, developed and deployed by the Administration of Union Territory, Chandigarh to modernise the administration of revenue courts.

Revenue courts adjudicate disputes arising from land records, tenancy agreements, agricultural holdings, and related matters. Prior to RCCMS, these proceedings were entirely paper-based — resulting in long delays, lost records, and limited public access to case information.

RCCMS replaces these legacy processes with a unified digital platform covering case registration, e-filing, automated cause-list generation, digital order publication, and real-time status tracking for citizens and legal practitioners alike.
